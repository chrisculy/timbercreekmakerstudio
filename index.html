About Us

We are Chris and Vanessa Culy, a husband-and-wife duo passionate about creating beautiful, handcrafted pieces that bring joy to your home. Our studio specializes in two distinct crafts:

Fine Woodworking

Led by Chris, our woodworking studio creates custom hardwood furniture and creative woodworking pieces that combine traditional craftsmanship with modern design.

Fiber Crafts
